John Molitoris Bio

John Molitoris is a young man that grew up in the heart of California. Raised on a small ranch in Alameda County with 2 sisters. Known to his friends as John-Jack, Joquin, Papa John, and of course as Jackie Jack Papa John. He loves the clear fresh air out in nature. Does Varsity Basketball and Throwing for the Granada Matadors. Favorite music genre is Rock.
